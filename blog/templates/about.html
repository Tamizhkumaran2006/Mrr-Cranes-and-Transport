About MRR Cranes and Transport

Company Overview

MRR Cranes and Transport is a prominent heavy lifting and transport service provider based in Chennai. With over 15 years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for construction, industrial, and infrastructure projects requiring specialized heavy lifting and transport solutions.

Our company operates from our main facility in Kannapalayam, Chennai, with service coverage extending across the Chennai metropolitan area, including Poonamallee, Avadi, and surrounding industrial zones. Available 24x7, we provide immediate response for both scheduled and emergency operations.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, efficient, and cost-effective heavy lifting and transport solutions that enable our clients to complete their projects on time and within budget.

Our Services

MRR Cranes and Transport offers reliable, safe, and efficient lifting and earthmoving solutions using our 14-Ton Hydraulic Crane and JCB Backhoe Loader. Our equipment is well-maintained and operated by certified, experienced professionals to ensure smooth execution of all on-site operations.
